International mobility (Second part)

11 weeks in Vietnam

For the second part of my mobility program, I had the opportunity to join Mercury Solutions in Hanoi, Vietnam, as a Full-Stack Developer intern. I contributed to the evolution of an existing web application, a CMS platform for Actiwell, by designing and implementing new features on both the front end (React.js) and the back end (PHP-Laravel). I worked closely with the development team to enhance the app’s performance, conduct software testing, and ensure product quality. Throughout this experience, I proposed innovative technical solutions that helped improve the user experience and even contributed to acquiring a new client for the company.
